# Problem Statement

## AI Personal Finance Tracker & Advisor

---

## 1. The Problem

Students and young professionals in India often struggle to track their daily expenses, leading to unintentional overspending in categories like **food delivery** and **entertainment**. Without clear visibility into their spending patterns, they miss opportunities to save and often exceed their monthly budgets without realizing it.

### Key Challenges:
- **No structured tracking** — Expenses are scattered across UPI apps, wallets, and cash with no unified view
- **Lack of categorization** — Users don't know how much they spend per category (food, transport, etc.)
- **No spending awareness** — Weekly spikes and category overspending go unnoticed
- **No actionable advice** — Even when users track expenses, they don't know how to reduce spending
- **Complex tools are intimidating** — Most budgeting apps require technical setup or paid subscriptions

---

## 2. The Solution

An **AI-assisted personal finance management system** that:

1. **Collects** expense data through a simple Google Form
2. **Stores** it automatically in Google Sheets
3. **Categorizes** expenses using ChatGPT/Gemini with structured prompts
4. **Analyzes** spending trends using rule-based formulas in Sheets
5. **Alerts** users when spending exceeds predefined thresholds
6. **Advises** with personalized savings recommendations
7. **Reports** monthly financial summaries via Canva

---

## 3. Target Users

- 🎓 **College students** managing limited monthly allowances
- 💼 **Young professionals** (first 1–3 years of earning) building financial habits
- 📱 **Anyone** who wants a simple, free, no-code expense tracker

---

## 4. System Scope

### In Scope:
- Expense logging via Google Forms
- AI-powered expense categorization (ChatGPT/Gemini)
- Rule-based spending pattern detection
- Budget threshold alerts
- Category-wise spending dashboard (Google Sheets)
- Monthly financial report (Canva)

### Out of Scope:
- Statistical financial forecasting or investment planning
- Real-time bank account integration
- Mobile app development
- Multi-currency support

---

## 5. Expected Outcomes

| Outcome | Measurement |
|---------|-------------|
| Accurate expense categorization | > 90% accuracy with AI prompts |
| Spending awareness | Users can see category-wise breakdowns instantly |
| Budget discipline | Alerts triggered when any category exceeds threshold |
| Savings improvement | Personalized recommendations based on spending data |
| Ease of use | Entire system operates with no-code tools |

---

## 6. Idea Behind the Project

The core idea is to demonstrate how **AI integration** (ChatGPT/Gemini) combined with **no-code tools** (Google Forms, Sheets, Canva) can solve real-world financial management problems for students. 

The system:
- Does **NOT** use statistical forecasting models or financial prediction algorithms
- **DOES** demonstrate structured AI prompting for consistent categorization
- **DOES** use rule-based financial analysis via Google Sheets formulas
- Provides budget recommendations based on **historical spending averages** and **predefined thresholds**

This approach is intentionally accessible — anyone can replicate it without coding knowledge.
