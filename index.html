⚙️ System Information & Project Limitations

This project is designed as a student financial tracking solution. Below are the documented system details and limitations:

  • Data Sync: Uses Google Sheets CSV export; updates may take 3-5 seconds to reflect after form submission.
  • Storage: Budget and manual amount overrides are stored securely in browser LocalStorage.
  • AI Logic: Categorization is rules-based with AI-driven pattern recognition for savings advice and trend detection.
  • Currency: Defaulted to Indian Rupee (₹) for student-focused budgeting.
  • Connectivity: Requires an active internet connection to fetch real-time data from the Google Sheets backend.
